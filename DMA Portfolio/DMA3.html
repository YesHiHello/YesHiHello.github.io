Artist Statement

Like any other fidgety kid in grade school, the only reason I picked up a pencil was because it was placed down in front of me and I didn't know what else to do with it. However, doodling my ideas suddenly became less of a pass time, and more of an experiment. I became infatuated with the goal of creating a monster, an environment, or an experience that would capture the eyes of like-minded dreamers and take them on a journey.
