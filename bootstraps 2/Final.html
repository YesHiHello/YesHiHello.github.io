This image set is a visual representation of how the mind deteriorates due to the effects of Alzheimer's and dementia. Using a digital art tablet and photoshop, I drew a portrait of my own family with my grandparents as the centerpiece. However this mainly focuses on my Nana, who lived with Alzheimer's in her final years. The slow decline in her memory showed often.
