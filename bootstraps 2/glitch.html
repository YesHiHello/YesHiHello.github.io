The Glitch Art project was an exploration and deconstruction of an image's source code. By copying, pasting, adding, and deleting lines of code, the original image becomes wildly or mildy distorted, creating sometimes terrifying or mesmerizing results. The top image of the crying cat meme was my first attempt at this method. While a majority of the image is still recognizable, the same can't be said of the second attempt. I took a screenshot of the Hylic's 2 Game Over screen and used this glitch method to deform the image. In contrast to the first attempt, the colors look much more interesting :)
